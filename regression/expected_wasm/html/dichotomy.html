Nonlinear equations solving with Dichotomy method

Function

$$\begin{array}{l} \textbf{function } f(x) \\ \left| \begin{array}{l} \quad \text{return } x^{3} - 8 \end{array} \right. \end{array}$$

Calculations precision

$$\epsilon = 1 \times 10^{-5}$$

Range

$$ \begin{array}{cc} a = -10 & \quad \quad b = 10 \\ \end{array} $$

Calculation:

$$n = 0$$
$$diff = \left|b - a\right|$$
$$\begin{array}{l} \textbf{while } diff > \epsilon \textbf{ do} \\ \left| \begin{array}{l} \quad x_{n} =\frac{b + a}{2} \\ \quad f_{xn} =f(x_{n}) \\ \quad f_{a} =f(a) \\ \quad product =f_{xn} \cdot f_{a} \\ \quad \textbf{if } product <= 0 \textbf{ then} \\ \quad \left| \begin{array}{l} \quad b =x_{n} \end{array} \right.\\ \quad \textbf{else} \\ \quad \left| \begin{array}{l} \quad a =x_{n} \end{array} \right. \\ \quad diff =\left|b - a\right| \\ \quad n =n + 1 \end{array} \right. \end{array}$$
$$x_{n} = \frac{b + a}{2}$$

Result:

$$x_{n} = 2$$

Left part value:

$$f_{xn,r} = f(x_{n}) = 3.43 \times 10^{-5}$$

Number of iterations

$$n = 21$$
